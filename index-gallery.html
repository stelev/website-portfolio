About Me

Personal Details

I began my journey to design through a rigorous high school program at the Teterboro Bergen Academy; my studies centered on Commercial Arts & Graphic Design. From there, I did my BFA at Rutgers University, centering my studies around Graphic Design, Silk Screening, Film Photography, and Illustration.

During the work day, I'm a multi-disciplinary designer with a passion for great type, doodling, strong kick-off meetings, and even stronger cup of coffee.

Out of work hours, I am an avid Redditor, crochet enthusiast, video gamer, and a true crime show binger. I love a good investigative series podcast and I love staying up to date on web trends.
